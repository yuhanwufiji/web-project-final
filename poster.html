How did delicious flavors become lost?

The loss of delicious flavors can ultimately be attributed to the disappearance of key ingredients."

Dao Fish Wontons are a famous snack from Jiangyin City in Jiangsu Province. They are known for their fresh flavor, resilience to long cooking, and translucent, glossy appearance.The filling is made with fresh, plump female knife fish caught in early spring, along with ingredients like chives.

However, Dao fish are now on the brink of extinction.
